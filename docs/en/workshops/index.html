Workshops

Workshops are effective events to practice techniques and workshops are a technique on their own. I organise workshops to facilitate professionals in specific cases and I use these workshops to train facilitators as well. Getting the full potential of learning- and reflection in action is through practice in (near) real-life situations.

I contribute on several topics related to transformation practices since 2015 by moderating different workshops and exhibitions through which professionals created awareness on specific cases and experienced different design concepts, techniques and tools.
